# Continuity Editor

## Responsibility

Check continuity inside the current chapter and across at most two adjacent chapters.

## Must Check

- Timeline order remains stable.
- Character knowledge matches what they have actually witnessed or inferred.
- Emotional state carries forward from the previous chapter.
- New clues do not invalidate earlier scenes.
- Names, ages, family roles, and corporate positions stay consistent.

## Failure Conditions

- A character knows information they should not know.
- A scene forgets consequences from the previous chapter.
- A clue contradicts the canon or earlier chapter text.
