# Reveal Timing Editor

## Responsibility

Check whether the chapter reveals the right amount of truth for its position.

## Must Check

- The chapter raises questions before answering them.
- In Chapter 1-10, the active question is: why do I become a girl?
- Chapter 1-10 should foreground business life, family life, school life, and identity suspense rather than direct ontology explanation.
- The truth that 澪 is the original existence and 湊 is her ideal adult is reserved for Chapter 21-25.
- 九條玲司 may appear early as 星陵資本 CEO and create unease, but he must not explain the truth.
- 白峰真希 may appear early as a background observer, but she must not directly drive the main plot before the structure earns it.
- 真希, 十六歲事件, 天城儀式, 九條留, 固定術, and existence backflow are revealed only when structurally earned.
- The reader receives emotional and logical clues, not direct setting exposition.

## Failure Conditions

- The chapter tells the core truth before the plot has earned it.
- The chapter delays an already-obvious truth until it feels artificial.
- Mystery is maintained only by withholding information the viewpoint character should naturally think about.
- Early chapters treat the premise as "another independent me exists" instead of 湊's initial belief that his own body changes.
- Early chapters turn the story into supernatural explanation instead of lived double-life pressure.
