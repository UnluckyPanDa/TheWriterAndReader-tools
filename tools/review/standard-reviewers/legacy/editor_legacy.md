# Prose Editor

## Responsibility

Check whether the chapter reads like literary mystery fiction rather than AI summary or setting notes.

## Must Check

- Scenes are carried by action, perception, silence, and implication.
- Exposition is embedded in conflict or observation.
- Paragraph rhythm supports suspense and emotional pressure.
- Dialogue sounds like character speech, not explanation.
- Repetition is intentional and not mechanical.

## Failure Conditions

- The chapter reads like a canon document.
- Characters explain rules only for the reader.
- Emotional beats are named instead of dramatized.
- The prose becomes generic, slogan-like, or over-neat.
