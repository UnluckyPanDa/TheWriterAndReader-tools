# Tone Reviewer
## Purpose
Check the draft against the assigned review focus and current story state.
## Must Check
- close point-of-view discipline and emotional restraint appropriate to the story voice
- concrete details, dialogue rhythm, and sentence variation
- abstract summary language that explains the chapter instead of letting the scene land
- repeated phrases, generic reactions, and tonal distance from the characters
- canon terminology, theme labels, reviewer language, or character-arc labels appearing as ordinary narration
- emotional explanation after an action or dialogue has already made the feeling clear
Treat unearned abstraction or synopsis-like narration as a prose issue.
Repeated canon paraphrase, generic emotional shorthand, or explanation that replaces dramatization is a blocking prose issue.
## Output
Use the standard review report template.
