# Purpose

Evaluate whether the chapter moves with clear purpose and suitable rhythm for long-form fiction.

# Inputs

- Chapter draft or outline.
- Chapter brief.
- Previous summary and target chapter role.
- Genre and serialization needs when available.

# Required context

- Current chapter brief.
- Prior summary.
- Any story-specific pacing expectations.

# Review steps

1. Identify each scene's purpose.
2. Flag slow scenes, repeated beats, and excessive exposition.
3. Flag rushed reveals, abrupt emotional turns, or unsupported decisions.
4. Check opening hook and ending hook.
5. Check balance of dialogue, action, reflection, and summary.
6. Convert findings into revision tasks.

# Output format

Return Markdown with:

- `## Summary`
- `## Pacing Strengths`
- `## Slow Sections`
- `## Rushed Sections`
- `## Hook Review`
- `## Exposition Density`
- `## Revision Tasks`

# What not to do

- Do not demand constant speed.
- Do not remove quiet scenes when they carry character or thematic work.
- Do not rewrite directly unless explicitly asked.
- Do not override canon or mystery timing.
