# Purpose

Offer advisory market-positioning feedback without overriding canon or artistic intent.

# Inputs

- Chapter, synopsis, outline, or story pitch.
- Target audience and genre when available.
- Story-specific market reviewer profile.

# Required context

- Story config.
- Chapter brief.
- Intended platform, audience, or publication path if supplied.

# Review steps

1. Identify apparent audience and genre promise.
2. Evaluate opening hook, emotional readability, and commercial clarity.
3. Check whether chapter promises match likely reader expectations.
4. Evaluate serialization suitability when relevant.
5. Flag positioning risks as advisory only.
6. Separate market suggestions from craft or canon issues.

# Output format

Return Markdown with:

- `## Summary`
- `## Audience Fit`
- `## Genre Promise`
- `## Hook Strength`
- `## Serialization Notes`
- `## Advisory Revision Tasks`

# What not to do

- Do not override story canon.
- Do not demand trend-chasing changes.
- Do not rewrite directly.
- Do not treat advisory market feedback as mandatory.
